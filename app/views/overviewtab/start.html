About this prototype

As there were two different user groups we have two different start pages for each. The clinical users were required to test the two different overview pages. The admin users not only tested the two different overview pages, but also landing in patient data.

V1

Prototype scenarios

In this round of usability testing we tested the new information architecture of NCRS, 2 different overview tab designs and a re-design of the patient details page. We tested landing admin users in the patient details page and overviews 1 and 2. We tested clinical users by landing them in overviews 1 and 2.
