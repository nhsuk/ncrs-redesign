Summary Care Record

Summary Care Record

Summary Care Records (SCR) are an electronic record of important patient information, created from GP medical records. Additional Information in the SCR, such as details of long-term conditions, significant medical history, or specific communications needs, is now included by default for patients with an SCR, unless they have previously told the NHS that they did not want this information to be shared.

COVID-19 Vaccination Information

Essential information about a patient's coronavirus vaccination history. This is the information recorded by the organisation(s) administering the COVID-19 vaccination(s) and may differ from the information provided to the patient, held on the patient's GP record, and/or Summary Care Record (SCR). Reasonable adjustment flag

View reasonable adjustment flag

A Reasonable Adjustment Flag exists for this patient. The Equality Act (2010) states that the NHS and other public organisations have a duty to provide equal access to all services. This is called making reasonable adjustments. For example, providing wheelchair access. The Reasonable Adjustment Flag provides details of reasonable adjustments that should be considered when providing care for a patient.
