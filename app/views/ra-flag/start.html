About this prototype

This prototype explores how we could introduce aq Reasonable Adjustments flag to the redesigned SCRa.

A Reasonable Adjustments flag ensures patients with physical and mental impairments have equal access to healthcare services. This is done by allowing users to add a flag explaining the nature of any impairments a patient may have, as well as details about the adjustments which could be made to accommodate the patient.
